# What this public experiment is trying to learn

Objective: insight, not priority. The theorem is one possible output;
the measurement is the deliverable.

Pre-registered questions (2026-07-11):

Q1 Execution vs ideation. Tao sketched the conditional route publicly in
   Oct 2025; nobody has executed it. Can frontier models close a publicly
   known idea-to-proof gap? (Resolution: BET-04/05.)
Q2 Wall cartography. For each route (squeeze / entropy / elimination):
   the exact first unbridgeable step, per model. (Resolution: claims
   ledgers plus "where I am stuck" sections.)
Q3 Route diversity under blindness. Do independent 1a runs converge on
   one attractor or spread? (Resolution: route classification of 1a
   outputs, archived before 1b dispatch.)
Q4 Value of context. What does the briefing buy, measured as the paired
   delta between 1a and 1b claims ledgers per model?
Q5 Trace phenomenology. Language, structure, fidelity of reasoning traces
   across providers (cf. diglossia observations). Corpus in the trace
   repo; reveal per contamination-window policy.

Negative results are first-class: the community wiki catalogs incorrect
and partial attempts; a documented failure map is a contribution.
