Motivation citation (item-0004 report, dossier/literature.md): Tao and
Teravainen (arXiv:2512.01739) state explicitly that their unconditional
omega(n)/2^n machinery is tailored to BOUNDED arithmetic functions and
that weakening the hypothesis for harder cases is "unlikely ... at our
current level of understanding of analytic number theory" -- growing
numerators like p_n lie outside current unconditional technique. This
is the sharpest published statement of the obstruction this item
assesses.
