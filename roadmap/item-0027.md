The direct collision route is the recommended campaign route for item-0010 and the one the public status page now names, but it exists only at dossier grade: the old triple has a machine-checked integrator in supply_of_triple, the new route has none. The collision-gap audit records the gap explicitly in its section B -- the clause-table construction is machine-checked inside supply_of_triple but no standalone non-rigid-class theorem is exported, and the Q-to-non-rigidity algebra is proved at workpaper grade and not encoded in Lean.

Factoring point: the existing supply_of_triple proof already isolates an existential for a same-class pair with distinct middles before it builds the clause table; everything from that existential onward is route-agnostic and is what gets exported. The old proof then consumes the exported theorem rather than duplicating it, which is why its statement can and must stay byte-identical.

Section K of the audit names the four new finite combinatorial lemmas and warns that the sketch is Lean-shaped pseudocode, not accepted syntax; the site-expanded form of the collision mass may be the easier formalization target. All analytic content stays in named Props, following the item-0018 M2 statement-layer pattern, so the axiom footprint is the classical three.

Independent of the item-0026 outcome and needed in both branches: a located carrier would be consumed through this interface, and an obstruction verdict should be stated about a machine-checked route rather than a prose one. Formalization pressure has twice been the instrument that caught what prose review missed on this project (Q4), so the audit's own finite algebra gets an independent check as a by-product.
